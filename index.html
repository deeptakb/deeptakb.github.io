Rajesh Singh


      

I am a final year graduate student in theoretical physics, working under the supervision of Professor Ronojoy Adhikari at the Institute of Mathematical Sciences, Chennai.

My PhD work is on studying the dynamics of hydrodynamically interacting colloids with active boundary layers.
