Welcome to my homepage!

Hi! I am graduate student in physics at Institute of Mathematical Sciences, Chennai.

Currently, I am working in soft condensed matter physics. More precisely, I study the microhydrodynamics of active dynamical systems.

My PhD advisor is Dr. Ronojoy Adhikari.
