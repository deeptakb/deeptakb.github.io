I am, Rajesh Singh, a graduate student in physics at Institute of Mathematical Sciences, Chennai.

Currently, I am working in soft condensed matter physics. In particular, I study the Brownian dynamics of hydrodynamically interacting 'active colloids.

My PhD advisor is Dr. Ronojoy Adhikari.
