About

My name is Rajesh Singh. I am a postdoc in the group of Professor Michael E. Cates at the Department of Applied Mathematics and Theoretical Physics, University of Cambridge. Prior to this, I completed my PhD in theoretical physics, working under the supervision of Professor Ronojoy Adhikari at the Institute of Mathematical Sciences, Chennai. Read more in my CV.
